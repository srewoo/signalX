What happened

The Reserve Bank of India kept the benchmark repo rate unchanged at 6.5% for the eighth consecutive policy meeting, citing persistent food inflation despite easing core prices.

Key events

  • MPC voted 5–1 to hold; one member favored a 25bps cut
  • FY27 GDP growth forecast retained at 7.0%
