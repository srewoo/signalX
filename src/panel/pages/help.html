Getting started

Open SignalX by clicking its icon in the Chrome toolbar. It opens as a side panel beside the page you're reading.

The panel is per tab — each tab keeps its own view, so you can browse news in one tab while you work in another.

Headlines and search work immediately, with no account and no key.

Adding your AI key (BYOK)

SignalX is bring-your-own-key: AI features (summaries, source comparison, and search overviews) run through an AI provider account that you own and control.

Supported providers

  • Anthropic Claude models
  • OpenAI GPT models
  • Gemini Google models
  • OpenRouter a gateway to many models

Where your key lives

Your key is stored locally on this device only, encrypted in Chrome storage. It is never synced and never sent to SignalX — it goes only to the AI provider you choose. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Add or change your key under Settings, AI Provider. Use Test key to verify it before saving.

Summaries & comparison

Open any story to generate an AI summary. Choose a length: Short, Detailed, or Key facts. Summaries stream in as they're generated.

Use Compare sources to see common facts, each publisher's perspective, and how coverage differs across outlets.

Each summary shows the model used and an estimated cost. Repeated requests are served from cache at no cost.

Give a quick thumbs up or thumbs down on any summary or comparison — your feedback stays on this device.

Bookmarks

Save articles and summaries to folders for later. Open the Saved tab, tap a folder to see what's inside, and use the trash button on any row to remove it.

Troubleshooting

When an AI request fails, SignalX shows a clear card with recovery actions. Your headlines and search keep working — only AI features pause.

Rate limited (429): your provider is throttling requests. SignalX retries automatically; you can also retry now or switch to a different model.
Invalid key (401): the key was rejected. Update it under Settings and use Test key.
Billing / out of credits: your provider account needs credits. Switch provider or open your provider's billing page.
Offline: SignalX can't reach the news sources or your provider. Check your connection and retry.

FAQ

Is SignalX free?

Yes. Reading headlines and searching the news is completely free. AI features (summaries, comparison, overviews) use your own AI key, so any AI usage is billed by your chosen provider — not by SignalX.

SignalX runs entirely on your device. See the Privacy Policy for how your data is handled.
